## Purpose

The **Income Tax Act** defines the structure and rates of personal and corporate income taxes in Samland. It outlines the rules for calculating taxable income, deductions, exemptions, and credits while establishing the obligations of taxpayers and the government. The act aims to ensure an equitable distribution of the tax burden, generate revenue for public services, and support economic growth by providing a fair and transparent tax system.
