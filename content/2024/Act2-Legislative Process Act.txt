SEC Purpose

  PARA 1

    The purpose of this Act is to establish the internal procedure for a formal legislative process in the Republic of Samland to ensure transparency, accountability, and the participation of elected representatives in the creation and amendment of laws.

SEC Proposal of Legislation

  PARA 2

    Any Member or group of Members may propose a new law or an amendment to an existing law.

  PARA 3

    Proposals shall be submitted in writing to the President, who shall ensure the proposal is properly documented and distributed to all Members.

SEC Review and Consultation

  PARA 4

    Upon receipt of a proposal, the President shall refer it to the appropriate committee for initial review and consultation.

  PARA 5

    The committee shall consist of Members and experts relevant to the subject matter of the proposal.

  PARA 6

    The committee shall conduct a thorough review, including public consultations and expert testimony if necessary, and submit a report with recommendations to the Government within 30 days of receiving the proposal.

SEC Debate and Discussion

  PARA 7

    Following the committee's report, the proposal shall be scheduled for debate and discussion during a formal session of the Government.

  PARA 8

    All Members shall have the opportunity to participate in the debate, raise concerns, suggest modifications, and provide input on the proposal.

  PARA 9

    The duration of the debate shall be determined by the President but shall not be less than two sessions of the Government.

SEC Voting

  PARA 10

    After the debate and discussion, the proposal shall be put to a vote by all Members.

  PARA 11

    A proposal shall be approved if it receives a majority vote of the Members present.

  PARA 12

    In the event of a tie, the President shall cast the deciding vote.

SEC Presidential Assent

  PARA 13

    Upon approval by the Members, the proposal shall be presented to the President for assent.

  PARA 14

    The President shall review the proposal and either grant assent or return the proposal to the Government with comments and recommendations for reconsideration.

  PARA 15

    If the President returns the proposal, the Government shall address the comments and recommendations and may resubmit the proposal for approval and assent.

SEC Publication

  PARA 16

    Once the President grants assent, the new law or amendment shall be documented and published in the official gazette of the Republic of Samland.

  PARA 17

    The publication shall include the full text of the law or amendment, the date of assent, and the date it takes effect.

SEC Monitoring and Enforcement

  PARA 18

    The Government shall establish a monitoring body to oversee the implementation and enforcement of new laws and amendments.

  PARA 19

    The monitoring body shall report regularly to the Government on the status and impact of the legislation.

SEC Amendment of Legislative Process

  PARA 20

    Any amendments to this Legislative Process Act shall follow the same formal legislative process as outlined in this Act.

SEC Effective Date

  PARA 21

    This Act shall take effect immediately upon publication in the official gazette of the Republic of Samland.
