PREFACE

  LONGTITLE An Act to provide for the development, maintenance, and regulation of public roads, ensure road safety, and facilitate the efficient movement of goods and people.

  This Act seeks to enhance the connectivity and safety of Samland's road network while promoting sustainable transportation practices.

PREAMBLE

  WHEREAS the development and maintenance of public roads are essential for economic growth and public convenience,

  AND WHEREAS it is in the public interest to regulate roads to ensure safety and efficiency.

  BE IT ENACTED by the Parliament of Samland as follows:

BODY

CHAP 1 - Preliminary Provisions

  SEC 1 - Short title and commencement

    PARA 1
      This Act may be cited as the Public Roads Act 2024.

    PARA 2
      This Act shall come into operation on 1st January 2025.

  SEC 2 - Interpretation

    PARA
      In this Act, unless the context otherwise indicates:

      SUBPARA (a)
        "road" means any thoroughfare, highway, street, lane, path, or other public way for vehicular or pedestrian traffic;

      SUBPARA (b)
        "Authority" means the "Samland Department of Transport" established under this Act;

      SUBPARA (c)
        "vehicle" includes all motorised and non-motorised conveyances permitted to use a road.

CHAP 2 - Establishment and Governance of Public Roads

  SEC 3 - Establishment of the Samland Department of Transport

    PARA 1
      There is hereby established the Samland Department of Transport, responsible for the planning, development, and management of public roads.

    PARA 2
      The Authority shall be led by the Secretary of Transport.

  SEC 4 - Designation of public roads

    PARA 1
      The Secretary of Transport may, by notice in the Gazette, designate any thoroughfare or proposed thoroughfare as a public road.

    PARA 2
      Before designating a road as a public road, the Secretary shall conduct public consultations and an environmental impact assessment.

CHAP 3 - Operation and Use of Public Roads

  SEC 5 - Regulation of traffic

    PARA 1
      The Authority may issue regulations to govern the use of public roads, including but not limited to:

      SUBPARA (a)
        speed limits;

      SUBPARA (b)
        permissible vehicle types;

      SUBPARA (c)
        restrictions on overtaking;

      SUBPARA (d)
        emergency lane use.

CHAP 4 - Safety and Environmental Provisions

  SEC 6 - Road safety measures

    PARA 1
      The Authority shall implement safety measures on public roads, including:

      SUBPARA (a)
        installation of safety barriers and lighting;

      SUBPARA (b)
        establishment of emergency response services;

      SUBPARA (c)
        periodic safety audits.

  SEC 7 - Environmental considerations

    PARA 1
      The Authority shall ensure that road development adheres to environmental standards, including:

      SUBPARA (a)
        measures to minimise habitat disruption;

      SUBPARA (b)
        provisions for wildlife crossings;

      SUBPARA (c)
        reduction of carbon emissions through sustainable construction practices.

CHAP 5 - Development and Funding

  SEC 8 - Road development plans

    PARA 1
      The Authority shall prepare a comprehensive road development plan every five years.

    PARA 2
      The plan shall identify priority projects, estimated costs, and timelines, and shall be subject to parliamentary approval.

  SEC 9 - Funding mechanisms

    PARA 1
      The development and maintenance of public roads shall be funded through:

      SUBPARA (a)
        allocations from the national budget;

      SUBPARA (b)
        public-private partnerships;

      SUBPARA (c)
        international loans and grants, where applicable.

  SEC 10 - Transparency and accountability

    PARA 1
      The Authority shall publish an annual report detailing road expenditures, revenue, and progress on development projects.

    PARA 2
      The report shall be made publicly available online and in the Gazette.

CHAP 6 - Technological Integration

  SEC 11 - Intelligent Transportation Systems

    PARA 1
      The Authority shall deploy Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS) to enhance road efficiency and safety, including:

      SUBPARA (a)
        real-time traffic monitoring;

      SUBPARA (b)
        electronic signage for dynamic traffic management;

      SUBPARA (c)
        systems for reporting and responding to incidents.

  SEC 12 - Data protection

    PARA 1
      All data collected through ITS shall be handled in compliance with applicable data protection laws and regulations.

    PARA 2
      The Authority shall implement measures to prevent unauthorized access or misuse of data.
